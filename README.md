
# Product Articles: PRD Repository

This repository is a collection of Product Requirements Documents (PRDs) and related product strategy articles compiled from my product work. It serves as a reference and knowledge base for product managers, developers, and stakeholders interested in structured product planning and documentation best practices.

## Overview

Each article and PRD in this repo documents a specific product feature, enhancement, or project. The documents cover problem statements, user needs, functional requirements, success metrics, and strategic context.

This repo aims to:

- Showcase practical examples of effective PRD writing
- Share lessons learned and insights from product strategy
- Provide templates and guiding principles for creating your own PRDs
- Foster a culture of clarity and alignment in product development

## Repository Structure

- `/prd` – Contains individual Product Requirements Documents in Markdown or PDF format
- `/articles` – Product strategy essays, best practices, and analysis
- `/templates` – Reusable PRD templates and checklists
- `/resources` – Supplementary material such as research data, diagrams, or reference links

## Usage

- Browse or search documents by product area or theme
- Adapt templates to streamline your own product documentation
- Learn from examples to improve clarity, scope definition, and stakeholder communication
- Contribute new PRDs and articles based on your product experiences
